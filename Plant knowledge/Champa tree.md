# Champa tree

Created: 29/01/2026 18:23

Scientific name: Magnolia champaca/Plumeria
Local name: चम्पक/चम्पा
Native to: South East Asia, Southern China
Type: सदाबहार / Evergreen
Sightings: I saw this at Hari Da's(Music/tabla) house

## Uses
1. Fragrance - Jean Patou's famous perfume, '**Joy**', the second best selling perfume in the world after **Chanel No. 5**, is derived in part from the essential oils of champaca flowers.
2. Timber for furniture
3. Ornamental - Abundant in Buddhist monastaries
