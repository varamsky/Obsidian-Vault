# Kachnar

Created: 29/01/2026 19:14

#tree #kachnar-tree

[[Kachnar_tree.jpg]] [[Flowers_of_Kachnar.jpg]] [[Camel_foot_leaves_of_Kachnar.jpg]]

Scientific name: Bauhinia variegata
Local name: कोविदार, कोइरालो, कचनार, मन्दारः, कांचन
Native to: South and South East Asia
Type: पतझड़ी / पर्णपाती / Deciduous or semi-deciduous

he seedpod dries completely on the tree, and when mature begins to twist into a helix or corkscrew shape, ultimately exploding open—with a very audible "clack"—to deliver its seeds into the environs.
## Uses
1. Ornamental
2. The tree is considered very sacred in Hinduism and it is symbolically depicted on the [[Kachnar_tree_in_Shri_Ram_Janambhoomi_Mandir_Dhwaja.jpg]] flag
3. Kachnar buds are also eaten as a stir-fried vegetable and used to make अचार. The achar is important part of the meal served during the [[Ghode Jatra festival]], Nepal
